Install the Skill

Afterself runs as an OpenClaw skill on your own machine. Your data never leaves your device. Install in one command and run the setup wizard.

Configure Your Will

Define your action plans; messages, transfers, account closures. Optionally enable Ghost Mode and let it learn your voice and writing style over time.

Afterself pings you periodically on WhatsApp, Telegram, or any channel. Just reply. If you stop responding, it confirms with your trusted contacts before activating.

Ethics & Safety

Technology around death
demands extraordinary care.

We'd rather ship nothing than ship something harmful. These are the principles we build on, and the lines we will not cross.

Consent Is Sacred

Only you can create your own Afterself agent. Ghost Mode requires explicit opt-in while you are alive. Nobody can create a ghost of you after the fact.

Transparency Always

Every Ghost Mode interaction is clearly labeled as AI-generated. We will never allow a ghost to impersonate someone without disclosure.

Your Data Stays With You

Afterself is local-first. Your vault, persona data, and voice samples live on your device. Nothing is uploaded unless you configure it.

The Living Come First

Ghost Mode exists to comfort, not to trap. Time decay is on by default. Beneficiaries can deactivate it at any time via the kill switch.

No Financial Exploitation

Ghost Mode has zero financial capabilities. We will never monetize ghost interactions or serve ads against conversations with the dead.

Dignity of the Deceased

The ghost will not hallucinate opinions the person never expressed. It represents who they were, not a continually-evolving fiction.

What We Will Never Build

A ghost that hides the fact it's AI A ghost created without consent A ghost with financial capabilities A ghost that evolves beyond real data A subscription model that monetizes grief Integration with advertising systems
